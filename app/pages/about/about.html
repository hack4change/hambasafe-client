Who

Hambasafe is a code collaboration between voltunteers who are hacking for change. The code is hosted on Github and anybody can contribute.

Why

Technology can solve many of today's problems. Let us use it to improve community safety.

When

The project was born during a 48 Hour Hackathon held at Workshop 17 at the V&A Waterfront, Cape Town, South Africa during March 18 to March 20, 2016.
